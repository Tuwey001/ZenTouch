Swedish Massage Details

Swedish massage is the most common type of massage therapy. It involves soft, long, kneading strokes, as well as light, rhythmic, tapping strokes, on topmost layers of muscles.

Duration: 60 minutes (extensions available)

Best for: Relaxation, stress relief, improved circulation

Deep Tissue Massage Details

Deep tissue massage targets the deeper layers of muscle and connective tissue. It's used for chronically tight or painful muscles, repetitive strain, postural problems, or recovery from injury.

Duration: 60-90 minutes recommended

Best for: Chronic pain, injury recovery, muscle tension

Aromatherapy Massage Details

Aromatherapy massage combines massage with essential oils extracted from plants. The oils are added to the massage oil to enhance the healing and relaxing effects of the massage.

Duration: 60-75 minutes recommended

Best for: Stress relief, mood enhancement, emotional balance

Hot Stone Massage Details

Hot stone massage uses smooth, heated stones placed on specific parts of your body. The stones may be used as massage tools or placed strategically while the therapist uses other techniques.

Duration: 75-90 minutes recommended

Best for: Deep relaxation, muscle pain, insomnia
